Rubik's Cube Club at Berkeley

Welcome to the website of the Rubik's Cube Club at Berkeley! We are a student group that promotes the growth of speedcubing in the Bay Area. We hold quarterly competitions and teach a Decal.
We have weekly meetings on Zoom at Wednesdays 8-10 PM, excepting days when there is no class. Anyone is welcome to drop in! Sometimes we talk about club logistics, but we mostly just cube.
Feel free to contact us at berkeleycubeclub@gmail.com for any questions.
