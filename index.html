Rubik's Cube Club at Berkeley

Welcome to the website of the Rubik’s Cube Club at Berkeley! We are a student group that promotes the growth of speedcubing in the Bay Area. We hold regular competitions and teach a Decal.

We have weekly meetings at Hearst Field Annex B5 on (some) Tuesdays from 8 - 9:30 PM (Spring 2025). Please go to our calendar to have details on exactly which weeks meetings happen, or join our Discord server for notifications. Anyone is welcome to drop in! Sometimes we talk about club logistics, but we mostly just cube. Official membership is reserved for UC Berkeley students and alumni.

To know what our events/competitions are during the semester, you can find them on our calendar link.

All our communication is through Discord. If you have any questions, please ask us there! We’re still a very active club, so we’ll respond very quickly there.

In case you don’t have Discord, feel free to contact us at berkeleycubeclub@gmail.com for any questions.

We are a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this website.
