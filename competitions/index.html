Competitions

We regularly hold competitions. You can find a list of competitions both in Berkeley, and in the general Northern California region on the West Coast Cubing website.

In Berkeley, we aim to hold at least 2 weekday competitions a semester, and at least 2 weekend competitions a semester, but this depends on room bookings.

We are a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this web site
